Volunteers

Old age homes are currently a residence to many elderly citizens and require volunteers to spend quality time with them and contribute to their well-being and good-health. Description:
Purpose of volunteers: To provide some quality time with senior citizens to bring a smile on their face.

Prerequisites
Time Duration: Flexible
Time Commitment: 3-4 hours a day
Key Responsibilities: Volunteer must be willing to spend a day with elderly people
Volunteers must provide emotional and physical support
Volunteer must talk, share conversations and play games with them
Skills and Experience Required: Volunteer must have a positive attitude.
Be able to provide warmth and empathy to patients
Communication skills
